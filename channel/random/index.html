I found Cargille really expensive too, until I found this supplier in the UK who sells 480 mL of type FF for 161 GBP, 322 GBP/L.

Not sure if that is helpful, I use it for CUBIC cleared samples.

Other people suggested mixing silicone and mineral oil at 1:1 ratio, which is probably the cheapest for the higher RI of ~1.48 for CUBIC

You can try 2,2-thiodiethanol, it mixes with water to make different refractive indices, you might need a refractometer to confirm the final RI you get (see attached graph)

*Thread Reply:* In brief it takes about two hours to stabilise in the direction RT -> 18C. it will be faster to go from RT to 37C, but while the temperature is set fast in the small incubator box inside the machine, the machine continue to warm up in its whole and this can led to drifting over several hours. You can attempt to use the hardware autofocus to compensate that.
